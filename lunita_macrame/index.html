About Us

Lunita Macramé is a company that produces and markets unique handcrafted pieces, inspired by nature. Emerged from the challenging times of the pandemic, in a personal search by the entrepreneur for an art that connected her to people, with bonding with the sea, forests and other elements of nature, as a therapy to combat the stress of isolation.

Unique creations!

The creations are developed with a lot of love and seek to convey to the public respect for the millenary macrame technique. The artist conveys her inspirations in each piece, always with the aim of producing in her audience, whether in their environment or in themselves, the positive feelings we feel when we are in contact with nature's creations. The pieces are braided with extreme technical rigor in the production of high-quality pieces, without losing the rustic character, typical of macramé.
